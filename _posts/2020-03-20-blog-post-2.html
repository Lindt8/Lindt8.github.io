--- title: 'A calculator for redistributing costs to make individual expenses equal' date: 2020-03-20 permalink: /posts/2020/03/cost_redistribution/ tags: - cost - calculator --- Provided below is a simple calculator for redistributing funds among a group of people. The general application is for scenarios like where a few people split the bill for a larger group of people and everyone wants to figure out how much they need to pay and to who to make everything even. Enter into the first field below the list of names of all participants, and in the second field enter the amount which has already been paid by each of the participants. Select the desired decimal precision of the results (useful if concerned about cents), and then click calculate to see how money should be transferred among the participants.
